An AI-Powered Collaboration Engine for VIT Chennai

VITsync is a strategic project designed to intelligently connect students for academic and project-based collaboration, overcoming the structural challenges of the Fully Flexible Credit System (FFCS) and fostering a more connected, innovative campus.

The Collaboration Gap at VIT Chennai

The innovative FFCS system, while offering flexibility, creates a significant challenge: a fragmented student network. Finding the right people for critical academic work is inefficient and often left to chance.

The Solution: Intelligent Matching

CogniMatch replaces guesswork with intelligence. Our platform provides two powerful, purpose-built tools to find the perfect collaborator.

1. The AI Discovery Feed

Our core feature is a personalized feed of recommended collaborators. The AI analyzes every student's profile‚Äîtheir skills, active courses, and project goals‚Äîto suggest the most compatible partners. Each suggestion comes with a "Match Reason," explaining why the connection is relevant.

> Reason: You need a React.js developer for your MGT1022 project. Anjali has this skill and is also in your course.

2. The Semantic Search Engine

For specific needs, our advanced search allows users to find partners using natural language. A user can simply type "looking for a machine learning expert" and the AI will understand the intent, finding profiles with related skills like Python, TensorFlow, and Data Science, not just the exact keywords.
